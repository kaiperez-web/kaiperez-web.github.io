All About Guitars

What Are Guitars?

Guitars are super popular instruments. You can play all kinds of music like rock, jazz, pop, and classical on them!

Types of Guitars

Some main types:

  • Acoustic: Makes sound naturally through its hollow body.
  • Electric: Needs an amp and can have cool effects.
  • Bass: Only has 4 strings and sounds deep and low.
