All About Guitars

What Are Guitars?

Guitars are one of the most popular instruments ever. You can play rock, jazz, pop, and even classical music on them.

Types of Guitars

There are a bunch of different kinds of guitars. The main ones are:

  • Acoustic: Makes sound naturally through its hollow body.
  • Electric: Needs an amp and can have cool effects.
  • Bass: Only has 4 strings and sounds deep and low.
