Types of Guitars

Through out the years multiple types of guitars have been made, 3 of the main ones that were made are acoustic, electric, and bass. Acoustic guitars made a sound that goes through the hollow body of the guitar natually. However Unlike the acoustic guitar the electric guitar requires a amplifier to make sound and also with that amplifier u make make the guitar sound different types of ways. Last but not least, Bass guitars are are guitars only with four strings that give out a deep tone through a amplifier.
