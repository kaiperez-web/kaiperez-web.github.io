Guitar

Types of Guitars

Throughout the years, many guitars were invented. The main types are acoustic, electric, and bass. Acoustic guitars make sound through their hollow bodies. Electric guitars need an amp and can make cool effects. Bass guitars usually have four strings and make deep sounds.
