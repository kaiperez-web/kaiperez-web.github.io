My name is Kailyne Perez. I am 14 years old and a freshman at Inwood Early College for Health and Information Technologies. When I first started here, I wasn’t sure what I wanted to do in the future and didn’t even consider college. Now, I am interested in studying criminology or criminal justice to become a crime scene investigator (CSI). I have several hobbies, including playing the electric guitar, acoustic guitar, drums, bass guitar, and keyboard. My goal is to improve my skills and learn more songs. Music has always been a passion of mine, helping me through tough times and inspiring me to possibly join a band someday. I also want to work to gain experience and earn money for the future. I enjoy playing instruments, exploring new places, listening to music, making beats, and talking with people. I consider myself a good listener who offers advice and can lift someone’s mood. I respect others’ boundaries and enjoy conversations about various topics. In middle school, I received over five music awards and was on high honor roll throughout seventh and eighth grade. I also had the chance to take the U.S. History Regents exam early. These achievements motivate me to keep improving and reaching for more.
